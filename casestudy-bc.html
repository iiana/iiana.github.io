Savings

When I worked on a discounted outdoor gear website, we had very low margins and as a result we couldn’t offer customers free shipping. This was a major pain point for our customers. They were motivated to find a good deal (which is why they were on a discounted gear site), which to them also includes free shipping. In reality, they were still getting a deal by shopping on our site.

To tackle this misconception, I wanted to highlight the amount that users would save by shopping at our website so they could have the shipping cost in perspective. This creates a relative value, which would help the customer make the choice to buy from our site and feel good about it. For example, if a customer sees that they are saving $100 by shopping at this website, the $10 shipping might not seem like a dealbreaker.
