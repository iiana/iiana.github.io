About me

Hi, I’m Iana - a UX designer based in Boulder, CO.

 Art and design have always been some of my top interests. From classical art to landscape design, I enjoy shaping people’s daily experiences. I consider myself a lifelong learner and love being challenged with new ideas and opportunities. That’s why I am excited to join a team of creative, smart people that I can grow with.

My journey has brought me to live across 3 countries and 14 cities. This variety has taught me that there are many different perspectives and ways of life. My main takeaway has been that nobody is always right, and everybody is right in their own way. I aspire to bring that takeaway with me wherever I go, hoping that it will help me to stay humble and never miss a chance to see the world from a new point of view.

In my spare time, I enjoy a good book and a cup of tea. I love nature and looking for the meaning of life. Let me know if you would like to chat over that cup of tea: iananoda.ux@gmail.com
